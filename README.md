# THE SPECTRUM · SPC

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **SCIENTIFIC**.

THE SPECTRUM — how atoms make light: an electron FALLING between energy levels sheds the gap as one photon of an exact wavelength — a spectral line. An interactive shows hydrogen's energy ladder (Eₙ = −13.6 eV/n²) and the visible BALMER series landing as real colored lines at their true Rydberg wavelengths (656·486·434·410 nm = H-α/β/γ/δ), the emitted photon traveling to a live spectrum strip; Lyman (UV) and Paschen (IR) flagged off-window. ⚑ Closes the s/p/d/f NAMING STORY: the orbital letters come from how 19th-century spectroscopists sorted these line-series by appearance — Sharp, Principal, Diffuse, Fundamental — then ran the alphabet (g,h,…); the lines came first, the structure was read out of them. Two-layer honest: wavelengths are exact hydrogen physics, the colour mapping is a standard approximation, multi-electron atoms split/shift the lines. This page is the MOTION (transitions); the rest of the cluster is the COUNT — the same ladder, the rungs vs the falls between them. With [[atomic-structure]].
